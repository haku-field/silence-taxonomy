# Terminology Guard

This appendix exists to prevent
terminological drift and reinterpretation.

It does not introduce new definitions.

---

## Guarded Terms

The following terms must retain their meanings
as defined in upper or sibling fragments:

- silence
- phase
- relationship
- preservation
- continuity
- non-intervention

silence-taxonomy must not
redefine or embellish these terms.

---

## Prohibited Reframings

The following reframings are disallowed:

- silence as intent
- silence as decision
- silence as protection
- silence as signal
- silence as authority

Such reframings collapse phase into action.

---

## Usage Constraint

Terminology in this repository is:

- descriptive, not prescriptive
- relational, not functional
- referential, not interpretive

If a term requires justification,
it has already drifted.
