# Maintained Conditions During Silence

This document describes **what remains valid**
while a silence phase is active.

Silence does not suspend relationships.
It preserves specific conditions without advancing them.

---

## Relational Continuity

During silence:

- The relationship remains valid
- Presence is maintained without exchange
- No signal of withdrawal or rejection is implied
- Future interaction remains possible

Silence holds continuity without requiring confirmation.

---

## Openness and Non-Finality

During silence:

- Positions remain non-final
- Interpretations are not closed
- Commitments are not demanded
- Alignment is not enforced

Silence keeps space open rather than filled.

---

## Non-Coercive Pacing

During silence:

- No response is required
- No timing expectation is imposed
- No recovery signal is demanded
- No performance pressure is introduced

Pacing is allowed to self-regulate.

---

## Observational Integrity

During silence:

- Observation may continue without interference
- Differences may accumulate without commentary
- States are not converted into judgments
- Monitoring does not replace observation

Silence preserves minimal footprint.

---

## Boundary Reminder

While silence is active:

- No authority is exercised
- No correction is performed
- No protection is enforced
- No resolution is implied

Silence maintains conditions.
It does not act upon them.
