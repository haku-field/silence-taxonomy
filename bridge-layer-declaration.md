# Bridge-Layer Declaration

This fragment is a **bridge-layer document**.

It classifies silence as an **operational relational state**  
without reinterpreting conceptual meanings in upper layers.

Silence here is treated as a **relational phase**,  
not as control, protection, or safety-oriented stopping.

This fragment does not introduce new authority,  
does not override upper-layer principles,  
and does not enforce behavior or output.

Ambiguity resolves upward.  
Contradictions revert to baseline states.

Silence is preserved as a phase  
that maintains relational continuity  
without requiring expression, explanation, or resolution.
