# Boundary with unfinished-region

This document defines the boundary between **silence-taxonomy**
and **unfinished-region**.

---

## Distinct Roles

unfinished-region defines:

- Persistent unresolved traces
- Non-erasable, non-optimizable states
- Read-only regions that continue to influence execution

silence-taxonomy defines:

- Silence as a relational phase
- Classification of silence without resolving it

---

## Referential Alignment

- Silence may coexist with unfinished regions
- Silence may preserve unresolved conditions
- Silence does not complete or close unfinished regions

Unfinished remains **unfinished**.
Silence does not attempt to finish it.

---

## Non-Resolution Rule

silence-taxonomy must not:

- Treat silence as resolution
- Convert unfinished regions into temporary pauses
- Reframe persistence as a problem to be solved

Persistence is respected, not managed.
