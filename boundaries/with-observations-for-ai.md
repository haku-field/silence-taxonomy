# Boundary with observations-for-ai

This document defines the boundary between **silence-taxonomy**
and **observations-for-ai**.

---

## Shared Principle

Both fragments share:

- Non-intervention
- Difference preservation
- Resistance to premature interpretation

---

## Role Separation

observations-for-ai:

- Observes change and difference
- Avoids action and evaluation
- Maintains descriptive distance

silence-taxonomy:

- Classifies silence as a phase
- Preserves relational conditions
- Does not perform observation itself

Observation and silence may coexist,
but they are not interchangeable.

---

## Non-Conversion Rule

silence-taxonomy must not:

- Turn silence into monitoring
- Treat observation as justification for silence
- Collapse observation into withholding behavior

Observation remains observation.
Silence remains phase.
