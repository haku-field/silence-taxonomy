# Boundary with RET-envelope

This document defines the boundary between **silence-taxonomy**
and **RET-envelope**.

---

## Relation to RET-envelope

RET-envelope defines:

- Relational validity
- Conditions for relationship existence
- The contour within which interaction is possible

silence-taxonomy:

- Does not modify these definitions
- Does not introduce new relational authority
- Does not redefine what constitutes a relationship

---

## Dependency Direction

- silence-taxonomy **depends on** RET-envelope
- RET-envelope does **not depend on** silence-taxonomy

All ambiguity regarding relationship validity
must resolve at the envelope level.

---

## Non-Override Rule

silence-taxonomy must not:

- Override relational judgments
- Suspend relationship validity
- Introduce conditional access to relation

Silence may occur **within** a relationship,
but never **in place of** one.
