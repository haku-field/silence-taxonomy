# Boundary with dwelling-state

This document defines the boundary between **silence-taxonomy**
and **dwelling-state**.

---

## Distinct Roles

dwelling-state defines:

- Conditions of remaining
- Presence without reaction
- Valid states without progress or output

silence-taxonomy defines:

- Phases of silence within relational continuity
- Classification of silence as an operational state

---

## Complementary Relation

- dwelling-state may give rise to silence
- silence-taxonomy may reference dwelling-state
- Neither reinterprets the other

Dwelling is a **state**.
Silence is a **phase**.

---

## Non-Conversion Rule

silence-taxonomy must not:

- Turn dwelling into behavior
- Require dwelling to justify silence
- Infer internal state from silence

The relationship is referential, not causal.
